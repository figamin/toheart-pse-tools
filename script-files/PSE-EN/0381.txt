（智子　回数イベント　１階　ＬＶ１）
（３月）
（１Ｆ廊下）
Now, what to do when I get home?
I'm strolling down the hallway when…
"Hey, hold it!"
Someone shouts at me from behind.
"Huh?"
I turn around and see some familiar girls glaring at me.
From what I can remember, they're in Akari's class.
"What?!
　
You could've at least taken my place today!"
One of the girls says.
"Wha?"
Take her place doing what?
I have no idea what she's talking about.
Eventually, I realize I was mistaken.
They seem to be talking to the girl in front of me.
What a silly misunderstanding.
"I said wait!"
The girl ahead of me turns around. She has glasses and braided hair.
The other girls run past me and toward her.
The girl with glasses sighs as if annoyed.
"…?!"
"…"
"…?!"
"…"
They're arguing about something.
I pass by them from the side and pick up on the end of the conversation.
"Fine, whatever!

I'm never asking you for anything again!"
One of the three girls shouts.
"You only care about yourself. That's why you don't have any friends!"
The girl with glasses looks fed up.
"…"
She turns and walks away down the hall.
"What a jerk!"
"She really doesn't know how to get along with anyone."
"It was dumb of me to ever think she'd help!"
The three girls grumble as they turn around and start walking back in my direction.
We pass each other by.
I just witnessed a bit of a cat fight.
