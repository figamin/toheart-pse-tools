After school...
I have the misfortune of being on cleaning duty.
I'm in charge of the area around the shoe racks.
I take out a broom...
...and sweep here and there before deciding I'm done.
Ugh, that sucked.
I walk down the hall as I gaze at the view outside.
The scenery in the distance seems white and hazy.
The view would be better from the roof, but this is still pretty nice.
I aimlessly made my way here.
It would be pointless to take another step.
I climb up and down this hill every day.
I wonder if it's making my legs stronger.
I bet it would feel good to speed down the hill on a skateboard.
It's the night of March 11th.
I do some reading before I go to sleep.
Manga counts as reading, right?
