（下駄箱）
I spot Masashi at the shoe lockers.
As I am about to call out to him...
「佐藤く～ん＠」
Someone else calls out to him in a sweet voice. I look over and
Masashi is surrounded by three girls.
"What for?"
Masashi turns around and asks.
「ねえ、佐藤くん。
今度の日曜日、私たちといっしょにカラオケ行かない？」
Female Student A asks.
"Come on, let's gooo!"
Female Student B tugs at his hand.
"You're free on Sunday, right?"
Female Student C asks.
"Aren't we in the middle of finals next Sunday?"
"It'll be a good break from studying!"
「そうそう」
"We know you don't have to study too hard to do well on your exams!"
"Sorry, I'm actually really feeling the pressure this time."
Masashi tries to decline without giving a straight reason.
But the girls won't let him get away that easily.
"Come on, let's go!"
"Let's go, let's go!"
"We've got a coupon that's valid until Sunday."
They are stubbornly insistent.
"Sorry, but I really do have to study that day. So maybe next time..."
"Aww, what? Now what should we do?"
"What should I do?"
「う～ん」
The three girls discuss amongst themselves.
They don't care whether Masashi is free or not.
"I'll totally make time to hang with you guys soon."
Masashi tries to smooth things over with a compromise.
"Well, whatever.
じゃ佐藤くん、また今度ね。
約束よ」
"Yup, sure. Sorry, but I'm in a rush."
Masashi forces himself to smile as he runs away from the girls.
「じゃ～ね～＠」
They wave at him.
"What?
おい、雅──」
I try to call out to him, but Masashi had already gone out the door
and left the school building.
I'd miss my chance.
Man, Masashi's really popular with the girls.
