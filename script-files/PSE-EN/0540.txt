Lunch Break
The weather's amazing with skies as clear as the eye can see. I
decide to head out to the courtyard for the first time in a while,
sit on a bench, and have lunch there.
I'm alone today.
　いつもは雅史といっしょに食ってるが、あいつはときどき、姉の手作り弁当を持参してくることがあり、そんなときは自然と別々に食うことになる。
　パンがふたつにパックのカフェオレ。
Just a regular lunch. Nothing special.
But the cafeteria is always filled with people and isn't the best
place to have a quiet lunch.
Lunch is a meal I want to relax and enjoy.
I rip open the sandwich wrapper as I bask under the glorious
sunshine.
As I stab the straw into my coffee and take a sip, I think to
myself that it feels pretty nice to have lunch alone every now and
then, until...
"...Oh, Fujita."
Aoi happens to show up.
She's holding a bag with her boxed lunch inside it.
"Are you having lunch on your own?"
"Yeah, I guess... You too?"
「はい、いつもいっしょの友だちが今日はお休みで。
あの、ごいっしょしてもよろしいですか？」
"Oh, of course. Go right ahead."
"Thank you very much."
Aoi sits next to me.
"Do you often have lunch here?"
"No, I usually eat in the classroom, but I figured the weather was
nice today."
"Absolutely.
せっかく、こんなにいい天気だし、外に出ないともったいないですよね」
