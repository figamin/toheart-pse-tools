I am painfully aware of Ayaka's strength.
As I gained more knowledge, I was better able to quantify my own
abilities and at the same time, understand her prowess,
the speed of her attacks and the dexterity of her footwork.
She would move to a good spot, throw in a feint or two, and then
unleash an effective combination.
She is good.
There is no way I can mimic her movements at my current level.
The more I try to get closer, the farther away she feels.
But that is just fine.
The goal is for an amateur like me to get a point off an expert
like her.
That's what makes this challenge meaningful.
Having a sky-high goal will give me more motivation.
