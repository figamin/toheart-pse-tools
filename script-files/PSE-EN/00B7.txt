（４月１７日）
（自室）
Thursday, April 17th.
It's morning already?
The earthquake last night cost me ten minutes of precious sleep.
"Hey, did you know there was an earthquake last night?"
"Yeah,
my mom told me when I woke up."
"What?
You didn't feel it?"
"Nope,
I was fast asleep."
"It happened right before I went to sleep.
I wasted ten minutes I could've spent asleep watching the news about it."
"Ten minutes is a lot for you."
"Seriously."
