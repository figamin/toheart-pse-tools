（智子　好感度イベン
ト１０回目）
（土曜日以外）
（好感度が　２５のとき）
（好感度イベント９を見たフラグがＯＮの場合）
（４時間目の授業中）
It's fourth period. We're in English class.
"Which of the following vocabulary words means…"
My tired eyes gaze out the window.
*sigh*
It's the peak of spring and the weather is phenomenal.
Another tranquil day.
On days like these, I could fall asleep if I'm not careful.
Gentle wind blows through a window, further tempting me to sleep.
"…"
Uh oh...
My eyelids are getting heavier.
"So this here…"
*scrtch, scrtch*
The teacher reads from the text,
and the sound of chalk against the blackboard lulls me like a lullaby.
I sleepily look around and find that I'm not the only one on the verge of dozing off.
Some yawn with their heads down, and some are even fast asleep already.
Given the circumstances...
What difference would it make if I took a nap too?
"*yaaawn*"
When I let my guard down and let out a big yawn...
"!"
I have the back luck of making eye contact with the teacher.
Oh crap!
I frantically stifle the yawn and shut my mouth, but it's too late.
"<family>!"
"Ugh…"
I reflexively hide my face behind my textbook.
"Too late, I heard that yawn.
Stand up!"
"Ugh..."
The class laughs at me as I rise from my chair.
Damn.
They sure seem awake now.
"Translate the sentence I just described.
You should be able to if you're prepared."
"Y-Yes, well..."
I smile tensely.
Man...
My seat number wasn't scheduled to be called on today, so I'm not ready.
"Read the following vocabulary word aloud and then define it for the class."
"Um, 'pernicious.' That means..."
Uh...
What is "pernicious"?
Pernicious...
It doesn't sound like a nice word...
Pernicious...
It's definitely an adjective...
Does it mean "picky"?
What the hell?
"Get on with it.
　
Now!"
God damn it.
I have no idea.
Can anyone help me?
Masashi?
He's too far away.
Akari?
Also far away.
Err…
Maybe I'll just bet on the first thing that comes to mind.
"What's wrong? Give up?"
"Uhhhhh..."
I'm about to give it a shot when...
The Class Rep casually hands me a piece of paper from the next seat over.
She'd written what seems to be a definition.
Oh, thank god!
I can use this.
"Having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way.."
"Alright,
that took longer than it should have, but you got it.
Sit down."
"Phew…"
I sit down and sigh.
I was saved.
The Class Rep doesn't fail to impress.
Apparently, her answer was perfect.
I look at the piece of paper again.
The pretty handwriting is written faintly with a mechanical pencil.
The mechanical pencil seems to use hard lead.
I cover my mouth with my hand.
"Thanks."
I whisper.
"…"
Rather than answer, she holds another piece of paper out to me.
What?
I take it and unfold it.
'You don't even know what pernicious means? How are you going to pass the class?'
"…"
Ugh…
She always has to be harsh.
"My bad."
When I say that...
"…"
She looks right at me and giggles.
