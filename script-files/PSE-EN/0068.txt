４／２５（金）
Another day's hard work over with,
I guess.
Now it's time to have some fun.
The second floor hallway is crowded with students leaving to go home.
I think I might run into someone I know, but unfortunately I don't.
I reach the shoe racks.
I look for love letters
but don't find any.
Oh well.
I leave school.
I guess I'll head home...
That night...
I get in bed early and close my eyes.
"Meooooooooooooooooow!"
I hear a cat.
It's nearby.
Meooooooooooooooooow!
And it's really loud.
Meooooooooooooooooow!
"…"
Meooooooooooooooooow!
"…"
Meooooooooooooooooow!
"Shut up!"
I jump up and shout.
A black cat is sitting on the fence.
It looks at my face.
Meooooooooooooooooow!
Then it starts to meow as if trying to communicate something.
Meooooooooooooooooow!
"Please, if you want to meow, do it somewhere else."
Meooooooooooooooooow!
"Shoo, shoo."
I try to drive it away, but it just keeps staring at me curiously and meowing.
"Please."
Meooooooooooooooooow!
It's hopeless.
I almost look for something to throw, but I'd feel bad if I hurt the cat, so I just go back to bed.
I put headphones on and listen to a CD.
This should be good enough.
"…"
Now I can't sleep because of the music.
Can't anything just be easy?
