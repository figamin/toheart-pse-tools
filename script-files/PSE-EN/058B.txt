　前回の約束どおり、その日からオレは実際に格闘技の練習を始めることになった。

karate.
　いまでこそ、いろいろ混ぜ混ぜのオリジナルスタイルの格闘技をやってる葵ちゃんだが、幼い頃から習っていたという空手がその基本スタイルになっているという。
Aoi and I change into our gym clothes and head over to the training
grounds.
"Now then, I shall now explain to you the correct way to hold your
fist."
"How to hold my fist?"
"Correct.
ボクシングでは拳はインパクト時にだけ握りますが、空手は拳や手首を痛めないように最初からしっかりと握るんです」
"Haha."
"Starting with your pinky, curl each fingertip into the base of your

"Pushing the base of the fingers..."
"That's correct. Now, push your thumb against your index and middle
fingers firmly."
"Like this?"
"Yes, just like that."
It's like I'm holding my own fingers.
"The knuckles of your index and middle fingers are what actually

"I got it now."
"Also, when you throw a punch, don't ever bend your wrist as it will

「わかった」
She also teaches me the basics of how to stand and assume the

me everything from scratch in great detail.
"The shortcut to progress is to master the basics. Repeat the same
thing over and over again to teach your body how to move
efficiently."
"Over and over again..."
"Um, please don't view practicing the basics as a chore. Even the
most experienced fighters go back to the basics. It's within the
basics that you will learn everything."
「わかった」
　──基本の立ち方と構え。

　──基本の受け。
The jodan-uke, soto-uke, and uchi-uke...
　──基本の攻撃、素振り。
The chudan-tsuki, jodan-tsuki, and ago-uchi...
The practice is REALLY bland.
To be completely honest, I want to do more intense training like

On the other hand, I understand the logic that the basics are
what's most important.
Aoi is doing her best to teach me, so I should just listen to her
and follow her instructions.
