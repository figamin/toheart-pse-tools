　５時間目の休み時間…。
I slump with my head down on my desk, ready to sleep at the drop of
a hat.
I am super sleepy.
"Everyoooone! Big news, big news, big news!"
And then, a walking megaphone bursts into the classroom.
It was big-mouth Shiho.
I don't know how everyone but me has the energy, but they all
gather around Shiho to listen in on the gossip.
Shiho's extreme energy is like a scandalous headline—It makes
everyone eager and curious.
It is always a let down, yet they keep coming back for more.
They sure do like their gossip.
Or maybe they never actually believe her and just enjoy her stories.
That's probably it.
According to the Shiho, news that trickled into my ears, Lemmy had
to suddenly return to her home country.
Her home country... Is she going back to the States?
