（４月７日）
April 7th.
It's the last day of spring break.
I invite Akari to go check out the cherry blossoms with me that night.
Cherry blossoms fill my field of vision.
A multitude of lights glow brightly, bringing the striking pink to life against the dark blue backdrop of the night.
The cherry blossoms came a little early this year.
They're in full bloom.
Akari and I walk through a pink tunnel lit by pale light.
The wind sends petals falling like snow in a blizzard.
"Wow..."
Akari holds down her hair from being messed up by the wind and opens her eyes wide.
After struggling to find the right words to describe what she's feeling, she eventually just says...
"So beautiful…"
And she left it at that.
"Pretty nice, huh?"
"Yeah."
She squints and gazes up at the dancing petals.
I watch her, feeling glad that we came to see the cherry blossom on our own.
We originally planned to have our typical cheerful cherry blossom viewing with the usual crowd, Masashi and Shiho.
However, they both called me to cancel at the last second.
I considered canceling the whole thing, but Akari had already taken the time to make bentos for us, so the two of us decided to go on our own.
It was nice to be away from the noise this time. It was kind of chill.
"I'm glad we came."
Akari whispers.
"…"
What's going on?
As I watch Akari, I feel a warmth spread in my chest.
Cherry blossoms fill my field of vision.
The petals dancing in the wind...
Akari holding her hair down and squinting...
Moving slowly, I took a step closer to Akari.
Then I reached out to touch her hair.
"<name>-chan?"
The sound of her calling my name snaps me back to reality.
What am I doing?
"What is it?"
She asks with a soft smile.
"Oh,
you just had a petal in your hair."
"What?"
Akari uses her palm to brush the petal from her hair.
"Did I get it?"
"Hm?
Y-Yeah."
"Oh, there's one in your hair too.
I'll get it."
Akari puts a hand on my shoulder and stands on her tiptoes.
She strokes my hair to brush the cherry blossom petal away.
Akari's face is right in front of mine.
"…"
Unsure of where to look, I end up lowering my gaze to stare at my feet.
"There, got it."
"R-Right, thanks."
I look away.
"What's wrong?"
"What do you mean?"
"You're acting kinda jumpy."
"I think you're imagining things."
I clear my throat.
Later, we take a walk around the park before we sit on a bench and eat Akari's handmade bentos.
When we finish eating, Akari unexpectedly stands up from the bench and dances in the falling petals.
And then…
"Let's come again
next year."
She smiles.
I sip some hot tea from my cup.
"Sure."
I nod.
Akari stands with her hands clasped behind her back, gazing at the cherry blossoms.
Her hair is just a little bit different, but she somehow seems different from the Akari I knew until now.
"Your hair
looks good."
I murmur.
"What?"
Akari looks at me.
"I didn't hear you,
<name>-chan."
*slurrrp*
I sip my tea.
"What'd you say?"
*slurrrp*
"Hello?"
*slurrrrrp*
"Hello?!"
Ugh, quiet down.
"I said this tea is good."
*slurrrrrp*
"Yeowch!"
I suck down a bunch of hot tea and almost burn my tongue.
