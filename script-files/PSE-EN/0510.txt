Shopping Arcade
I'm wandering around the shopping arcade in the afternoon when I
see a familiar face outside the bookstore.
It's Aoi.
I approach her.
"Hey, Aoi!"
「あっ、先輩」
"What are you doing here? Don't you go to the dojo on Sundays?"
"The dojo's closed today. I was looking for a book I wanted at the
bookstore."
"A book? Is it about martial arts?"
"Oh, how did you know?"
「そりゃーな」
I can't help but smile.
"So did you find the book?"
"No, this store didn't get many copies, so I ordered it."
"Huh.
なんて本？」
"The Science of the Strongest Martial Arts."
"Interesting."
I can imagine what the book is about from the title.
"And why are you here?"
「オレ？
　べつになにも。
暇だからブラブラしてただけさ」
"On a nice day like this, I can see why you wouldn't want to waste
time sitting around in your room."
"Kinda."
Now that she mentions it, maybe that was why I'm out.
"What are you gonna do now?"
"I figured I might as well practice at the usual spot."
"Behind the school?"
"Okay..."
When she says that, I notice the familiar sports bag on her
shoulder.
Her towel and gym clothes are probably in there.
"You never skip a day, huh? How admirable."
"I just do it because I enjoy it. It's not that impressive."
Aoi blushes.
It's rare to run into Aoi in a place like this.
Maybe I should join her for practice.
