（智子　回数イベント　帰り道　ＬＶ１）
（４月）
（本屋内）
I stop by the bookstore on the way home and stand around reading a magazine.
When I look up from the magazine and glance outside...
"Hm?
That's…"
Class Rep happens to be passing by on the street in front of the store.
When I'm about to put the magazine back on the shelf and go talk to her, I notice that she's with someone.
It's a guy wearing another school's uniform. They're walking together.
Who's that?
Her boyfriend or something?
The guy is talking about something while Class Rep is listening with her typical poker face.
It doesn't seem like she really wants to be there, more like the guy is hitting on her and she can't get away.
I want to get a closer look at the guy's face, but they walk past the store and out of sight.
Hmmm…
I'm surprised to see her in this situation.
And now I'm curious...
A. Follow them.
B. Respect their privacy.
