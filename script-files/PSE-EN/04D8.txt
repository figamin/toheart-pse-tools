I spot Shiho in the hall during a break.
She's with Naitou and Nagata.
Just about everyone in our grade knows about the trio.
They're the very definition of modern schoolgirls.
They often hang out near the station after school.
They're probably always going to karaoke parties with students from other schools.
With how popular they are within our school, I assume they're pretty well-liked outside of it too.
Still, while Naitou and Nagata are one thing, I have to question why Shiho is so highly rated by the boys.
I guess that if you just saw Shiho walking around smiling, she could pass as cute.
It's what's on the inside that's the problem with her.
She's rude no matter who she talks to.
It'd be different if she could at least act cute around a guy she liked, but it doesn't seem like she can.
Personally, keeping enough distance from her to not get annoyed while staying friends seems the best approach.
"Hey, <name>."
As I'm thinking, Masashi approaches me.
"Hey, Masashi."
"Looks like you've got something on your mind."
"It's nothing much.
I'm just wondering why Shiho's so popular."
"That's simple.
She's cute, cheery, trendy..."
Masashi sounds sincere.
I'm stunned.
"Do you actually think that?"
Masashi shyly smiles.
"I at least think that's what most people think."
He says that with confidence.
"Don't scare me like that."
"But I'm positive that's what most people believe."
"But why? That's what I don't understand."
"Because she looks that way, I guess."
"That much is obvious."
I already know that.
"So that's what it is, then."
That's when I realize.
"Really?"
"Probably."
Hmmm...
Maybe it really does just come down to looks.
