４／２１（月）
After school...
I have the misfortune of being on cleaning duty today.
I'm supposed to clean up around the stairs on the second floor.
I take out a broom...
I sweep here and there with a broom before deciding I'm done.
Then I go back to the classroom.
What a pain in the ass.
I walk down the hallway and reach the stairs.
I decide to head home early.
I walk down the hallway and reach the shoe racks.
I pass by a teacher and get called out for slacking off.
I leave school and walk down the hill.
I wonder if anyone I know is walking home.
It didn't look like it.
I head home by myself.
April 21st is coming to an end.
Goodbye, April 21st.
We'll meet again next year.
