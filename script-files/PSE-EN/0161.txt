（あかりエンディング　２）
（５月６日）
（修学旅行の朝）
（玄関）
It's the morning after the long weekend.
The school trip is starting today.
I leave home much earlier than usual.
"Good morning, <name>-chan."
As soon as I leave, I hear a voice.
It's Akari.
She makes it seem like a coincidence, but I know she left home early to wait for me.
She has the same smile as always.
But her gaze is a little shaky, and I swear there are tears in her eyes.
"…"
She's about to say something, but stops herself.
She looks down and remains silent.
Akari...
I spend the whole weekend thinking.
What should our future relationship be like?
How do I really feel about Akari?
And the conclusion I reach is…
"Alright, then let's go."
I grin and pat Akari on the back.
"Wouldn't want to be late on the first day of the school trip."
"Oh..."
Akari opens her eyes wide.
"Okay!"
She nods emphatically.
Akari is Akari. The answer is simple.
I don't need to rush to decide whether she's my girlfriend or just a childhood friend.
We have plenty of time to figure it out.
So, I'll carry on as usual.
Whether our relationship stays this way forever or we become a couple one day, Akari will still be Akari.
I love Akari.
I know that much for sure, and that's enough for now.
What kind of love is it?
I'll figure that out later.
For now, all that matters is that I love her.
Four tour buses are parked in front of the school.
Kids are chatting at the school gate as they wait.
I put Akari's heavy luggage down and decide to sit on the ground rather than join them.
"Ugh, I'm tired...
This is all Akari's fault."
Akari's bag is needlessly heavy, so I carry hers while she carries mine.
That does me in.
The trip hasn't even started, and I'm already dead tired.
"This girl..."
I sigh and look at the sky. The dazzling May sunlight shines into my eyes.
