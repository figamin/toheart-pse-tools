（４月１４日　朝）
（自分の部屋）
Monday, April 14th.
The weather's still good.
And I'm feeling good too.
"Oh…"
At the school gate, Akari suddenly yelps and sticks out her tongue.
"What are you doing?"
A cherry blossom petal got in my mouth;"
She plucks out a pale pink petal and shows it to me.
"Just swallow it."
"I could never."
