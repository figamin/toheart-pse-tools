（レミィ　回数イベント　学校外１）
（校門２）
When I leave the school's entrance hall, I bump into Shiho.
「これはこれは…、<family>のダンナ」
She approaches me speaking in a weird fashion.
"What are you up to now?"
Shiho smirks.
"Fish sink, geese falter, the moon veils itself, and flowers bow..."
She says.
I can't help but shrink away.
「お前…中国語、うまいな…」
"What's with the look? What kind of bird brain are you?."
「う、うるせぇ！
　お前が気持ちの悪いこと言うからだ」
"Shut up. Fish sink, geese falter, the moon veils itself, and
flowers bow. Try saying it yourself."
「なんでだよ」
"Just do it! I'll yell if you don't."
I don't know what kind of threat that is, but she threatens me
anyway.
...Fine. She'll get off my back if I say it, right?
"Fish sink, geese... What was it again?"
"Fish sink, geese falter, the moon veils itself, and flowers bow!"
"Fish sink, geese falter, the moon veils itself, and flowers... bow!
There! I said it."
"Gosh, how forward of you! You're making me blush."
Bliss suddenly sweeps over Shiho's face.
「は？」
"Fish sink, geese falter, the moon veils itself, and flowers bow...
It's an old-timey way of describing a beauty that humbles nature
itself—my beauty."
"...Are you sure you didn't just make that up yourself?"
I don't know why, but she is sounding a whole lot like Lemmy.
"Ahh... It was as if those words were written for me..."
She speaks in a sing-song manner as she leaves.
Drunk on herself...
「バ～カ」
I mutter in the direction Shiho prances off to as I start to walk
myself.
"Oh, it's Lemmy."
I spot Lemmy walking way ahead of me on the sloped path.
She is the only person I can easily recognize from far away.
...What should I do?
Maybe I'll ask her if that weird phrase Shiho taught me is real.
　オレは──。
A. Chase after and call out to her.
B. Don't talk to her.
