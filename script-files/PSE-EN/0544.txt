"By the way, do you always have sandwiches for lunch?"
Aoi asks, looking at what I have in my hand.
"Yeah.
Both of my parents work, so my mom's too busy to make me lunch.
This is what I usually get."
"Is that so…"
She almost appears to feel sorry for me.
Alright, here's my chance to give something a shot.
"I mean... Sure, I'd love to eat a bento lunch lovingly made for me every now and then..."
I suggested with an "oh-woe-is-me" tone of voice.
"Senpai..."
"Man, I'm sure sick of eating sandwiches..."
I side-eye Aoi in the most obvious of ways.
"U-Um, well, if you don't mind my cooking. I could…"
"You'll make me lunch?!"
I say with the biggest, sparkly eyes ever, but then Aoi puts her hand over her mouth to stop herself.
"Come to think of it, I'm actually not very good at cooking at all."
Aoi says with a wry smile.
"Oh! I'll get my mom to…"
"No!"
I cut Aoi off.
"Cooking's not about being skilled or not!
It's about heart!"
"Cooking is about heart..."
"That's right. Having a heart that yearns for others to eat your cooking.
It's that motivation which brings out the best in cooking!"
"…"
After some thought...
"You're right.
Then I'll make lunch for you."
Aoi smiles.
"For real?!"
"Yes?"
"Yaaay!"
*doot-dee-doo*
I feel as if I just heard a celebratory trumpet flourish.
"But please don't expect too much of me.
I'm not great with my hands..."
"That's totally fine.
I'm happy as long as you're the one cooking for me."
Aoi smiles bashfully.
