（レミィ　回数イベント　１階　３／１３）
（選択肢　話す）
"Heya, Lemmy."
「<kata>、明日ダイジョブ？」
"About what?"
"Finals are tomorrow."
"Oh yeah, they are..."
I yawn as I answer, making Lemmy giggle.
「余裕だネ、<kata>」
She says.
"Are you kidding me? I'm always scrambling to make sure I get a
passing grade. No way in hell am I confident!"
"Really?"
"Yeah. I'm barely managing a pass."
She stares at me.
「<kata>って、成績良さそーなのに…」
Lemmy says.
"...I guess I'll take that as a compliment."
Nothing screams loser more than a dumbass who looks smart.
But "looking smart" is better than "looking stupid," I suppose.
"English is the only subject I get good grades in."
Lemmy says with joy.
I can't tell if she is being serious or not.
Well, it makes sense for her to do well on English exams.
"What about Japanese?"
"There are barely any questions about proverbs in our Japanese exams."
"Ohhh, right. You're good at proverbs but not necessarily Japanese.
Must be tough for an international student to take Japanese exams..."
"It's no different for me in the States."
She smiles unabashedly.
Lemmy always seems to have as much fun at school as someone does at
an amusement park.
"But unlike in Japan, there isn't so much stress placed on education
in America, right?"
"Japan can be pretty tough when it comes to school."
"The United States sure does sound like a nice place..."
　──その日は、他愛もない話題でレミィと廊下で長話をしてしまった。
　彼女とあれだけ長く話したことは、いままでなかったので、結構新鮮な感じだった。
