"I like your notion of striving to be yourself."
I say.
"The truth is that everyone has that resolve inside of them."
"What?"
"Everyone has their own set of values and while they should stick to them, they're sometimes unable to be upfront about acknowledging them because they don't want to stand out from the crowd."
"…"
"But you're able to assert yourself.

"R-Really?"
"Yeah,
I'd like to replicate that myself."
"G-Gosh, I'm really no one special..."

I end up staying with her as she trains until the sun sets, again.
