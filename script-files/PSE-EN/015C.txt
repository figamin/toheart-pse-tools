（あかり　好感度イベント１０）
（５月２日の朝イベントと差し替え）
*beep-beep-beep-beep* *beep-beep-beep-beep*
Morning has the nerve to come yet again.
"…"
Apparently, I fell asleep mostly naked.
I sit up in bed and gaze blankly at nothing.
My mind wanders back to what happened with Akari last night.
My heart is heavy with guilt.
How I wish it was a dream.
I clench my hands and my right fist aches.
It was from punching the wall last night.
I did that to myself, as stupid as it was.
May 2nd.
All I have to do is get through school today, and then it will be a three day weekend.
Normally, that would cheer me up, but...
I leave home ten minutes earlier than usual.
That's to avoid Akari, of course.
I don't want to see Akari for a while.
After making it through the day, it's a long weekend.
And then the school trip starts on Tuesday.
We won't have to face each other for some time.
I want to take some time to get my feelings in order.
I sit at my desk and gaze out the window.
"<name>-chan."
Akari arrives after me and approaches my desk.
"Good morning.
You're here awfully early today.
I was sure you were gonna sleep in again, so I stopped by your house first, and…"
"Akari."
I cut her off.
"Wh-What?"
"Sorry,
but can you let me be alone for a bit?"
"Sorry…"
After Akari leaves, I rest my head on the desk.
