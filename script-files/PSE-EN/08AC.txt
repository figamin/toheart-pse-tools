（レミィ　回数イベント　１階２　ＬＶ２）
（選択肢　海）
「──やっぱ、海かな」
I answer after some thought.
「<kata>、海が好きなの？」
"Okay.
海ってのは、とくに夏の海がワクワクしてくるな。
色がいいよ。
青い空…もっと青い海。
それから、入道雲と白い砂浜、カラフルなビーチパラソル──。
なんかそれだけでワクワクしてくるな」
I say everything that comes to mind as I picture myself at the
beach.
「うん」
Lemmy nods in response.
"Just that atmosphere alone makes it feel as though something fun
will happen... I'm totally a beach guy—the beach in summer in
particular."
...My reasoning ends up being based on abstract imagery.
「アタシ、海の近くで生まれたから、海のこと、特別に考えたりしなかったケド、…<kata>の気持ちを聞いて、アタシも夏の海、特別に思えるようになってきたワ」
"Oh, what? Beaches are commonplace for you because you were raised
in California? Geez, I'm super jealous of your perspective."
「アタシは海が好きな<kata>、うらやましいネ」
"Pfft. You're just trying to make me feel better about myself."
I say in feigned annoyance.
"Ehehe...
でも、ホントだヨ」
Lemmy smiles sheepishly.
「はいはい」
「…ねえ、<kata>」
Lemmy suddenly stops and calls out to me.
「なんだよ」
"Let's go to the beach in summer!"
"It's still spring. Summer's a ways away, you know?"
"C'mon, let's go!"
Lemmy is stubbornly insistent.
She leaves me with no other choice.
"Okay, fine. We're still getting a bit ahead of ourselves, but let's
go during our summer break."
I suggest a compromise.
"Really?"
"But not just us two. We'll invite a few others from school, okay?"
「うん！」
Lemmy and I continue the conversation as we walk home.
