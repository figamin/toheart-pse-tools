（あかり　回数イベント　２Ｆ）
（Ａ　相変わらずドジだなあ、と思った）
I think about how clumsy she can be.
And then, Akari says...
"Let me guess...
You were just thinking about how clumsy I am,
weren't you?"
Somehow, she's spot on.
"How could you tell?"
"I just could.
You always have that look in your eyes when you call me clumsy."
"'That look?'
What look?"
"When you say I'm clumsy, your eyes say 'whatever.'"
My eyes say whatever?
Is that how I look when I look at Akari?
"I see. That's observant of you."
I answer.
"Well, I have known you for sixteen years, remember."
Akari smiles.
We look at the rainbow until it disappears, and then we walk home together.
