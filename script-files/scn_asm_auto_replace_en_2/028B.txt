（選択肢Ｂ　先輩に呼びかける）
Kurusugawa gets in the limo.
"Kurusugawa!"
I can't stop myself from crying out to her.
She jumps and looks in my direction.
"Let's go home together next time."
"…"
She stares at me and nods.
*clack*
But the closed door severs the connection between us.
"Oh?"
The limo quietly speeds off.
"Bye, Kurusugawa."
I mutter at the speeding car.
Kurusugawa watches me from the back seat. It's like she feels awful about what just happened.
