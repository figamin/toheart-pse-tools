（智子　日付イベント　１回目）
It's the morning of April 9th.
（必ず発生）
（１時間目の授業中　教室）
The first period of the day, in other words, the first class of the
new school year, is homeroom.
Our new homeroom teacher is Mr. Kibayashi.
"Alright, first you all need to introduce yourselves. Let's start
from this side."
And so, the first thing I have to endure is the other kids' boring
self-introductions.
Everyone lists off their name, previous class, after school clubs,
and hobbies.
Still, there are over forty students in this class.
So, there's no shot that I'm remembering all of this.
Oh!
Looks like Masashi's up next.
「…佐藤雅史です。
一年の時はＡ組でした。
サッカー部所属です。
…趣味は、ええっと、ハムスターの飼育とか──」
A few girls giggle.
"Hey, is he joking?"
"I'm not sure."
I hear them whisper.
Nah, it's no joke.
Masashi never jokes about himself to get laughs.
He's still got a smile plastered on his face, but I doubt he even
knows what the laughs are about.
He's always been sort of oblivious in some ways.
Masashi does actually like to take care of hamsters and other such
pets.
　たしか幼稚園の頃から、ずーっと欠かさずに飼い続けてるんじゃなかったっけ。
We always had him take care of the class pet in elementary school
because of that.
As these thoughts passed through my head, my turn eventually came
around.
What should I say?
