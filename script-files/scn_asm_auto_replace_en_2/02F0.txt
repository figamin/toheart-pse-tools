I go with Kurusugawa to buy 'The Origins of Demons in Grimoires.' Afterwards, we leave the used bookstore together.
"Huh, so you can pay with a card here?
That's pretty modern for a shop run by an old guy."
"…"
"What?
He sets aside books that you're interested in buying?
He sounds nice."
She nods.
I guess he really is a nice guy.
"Do you come to this bookstore often?"
She nods.
"Huh, cool.
Is the old man into the same stuff as you?"
"…"
"He's a member of a magic association?
That's a thing?"
She nods.
"Anyway, it's not often that you're by yourself.
How about you and I go and―"
"It's time to go, Lady Serika."
Just then, a man in a dark suit, the most stereotypical butler I'd ever seen, came out of nowhere.
"…"
"What?
You have to go?
Alright,
that's too bad."
"…"
"Well, until next time, then."
Kurusugawa looks a little disappointed as she leaves.
All that remains of her is the subtle scent of her hair.
The butler escorts Kurusugawa to the black limo.
She glances back at me as it starts to drive off. I smile and wave.
Hmmm...
I guess rich girls have to follow a strict schedule.
I kinda feel bad for her.
