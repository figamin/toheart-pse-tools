（Ｂ　恋人としては見ていない。）
"I'm sorry, but I just can't see you as girlfriend material, Rio."
I lower my gaze and mumble.
「そう…」
Rio looks down too.
「うん、いいよ、それでも。
ただのお友だちでも、いつも楽しいもの」
"You sure you're okay with that?"
"Sure I'm sure! I'm not disappointed at all. I mean, look how far
we've come!"
I wonder if she really feels like that.
In any case, I am glad she took it so well.
「だから、そのままでいいと思う。
でも、ごめんね、へんなこと言わせちゃって」
She quickly turns around.
"I have to go now. See you around!"
I watch her walk away with a dull pain in my heart.
