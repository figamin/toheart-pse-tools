（芹香　回数イベント　１Ｆ　ＬＶ１）
（選択肢Ｃ　毎日いいもん食べてんの？）
"Do you eat fancy food every day? I bet you do."
I ask.
"What?
You're not sure if the food you eat is fancy?
I guess you'd have nothing to compare it to."
"…"
"What?
What kind of food do I eat? 
Well, for example…"
Wait a second, what do I eat?
I try listing some things I'd eaten recently.
"How about rice with egg and grated yam on top.
You eat that?"
She shakes her head.
"No?
Then what about nameko mushrooms with grated daikon?"
She shakes her head again.
"No?
Seriously?
What about boiled tofu?"
Another no.
"You've never had boiled tofu?
I almost feel bad for you."
"…"
She looks sad.
"Well, in that case... I'll teach you about some commoner food next time I have the chance."
"…"
"What?
You like that idea?
Alright, you can count on me.
I'll even cook for you."
She nods twice this time.
I guess she really did like my suggestion.
