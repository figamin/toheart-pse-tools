４／１２（土）
After school...
It's, but I have the misfortune of being on cleaning duty today.
Not only that, but I have to clean the entire first floor hallway.
I grab a mop and get to work.
I clean every inch of the floor.
There, all done!
I return to the classroom.
Ugh, I'm starving.
I walk down the hallway and reach the stairs.
I decide to head home early.
I walk down the hallway and reach the shoe racks.
I pass by a teacher and get called out for slacking off.
I leave school and walk down the hill.
I wonder if anyone I know is walking home.
It didn't look like it.
I head home by myself.
That night...
I eat, take a bath to clean myself up, and laze around watching TV
in the living room.
It's getting late, so I get in bed and go to sleep.
Goodnight.
Then, April 12th passes by.
