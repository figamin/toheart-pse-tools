The weather is beautiful, so I go out on a walk around the
courtyard when I spot Kotone sitting by herself on a bench.
"Hey, Kotone."
"Oh!"
She hears me, stands up from the bench, and bows her head slightly.
"...Hello."
"Out soaking up some rays too?"
"Um, yes."
"Mind if I sit next to you?"
"Oh, uh, go ahead."
I speak in a pretty casual tone while Kotone is on the polite side.
It wouldn't hurt for her to be a little less cold towards me.
I sit down on the bench.
"You should have a seat too, Kotone."
"Uh, okay."
Kotone nods and sits down next to me with a slightly nervous look
on her face.
"Do you always come here at lunch time?"
"No, I just ended up here... I was walking to somewhere with fewer
people and...that's how..."
She trails off.
It is as if she has said too much.
"She seems to find it suffocating in her own classroom, as usual.
Her classmates will go out of their way to avoid her like the
plague because of her supernatural abilities.
So she will go find isolated places to be by herself before other
students can judge her.
I feel sorry for her.
I don't care about any of the rumors.
But I decide to avoid the topic.
"Hey, since we're here in a quiet place, let's talk about something
fun!"
I smile.
「…え、ええ」
Kotone nods awkwardly.
There is a slight look of uncertainty on her face that she can't
hide.
She is probably worried about her supernatural abilities causing me
trouble.
"Nice weather, huh?"
"You're right about that."
Hmm, what should we talk about?
We engage in some small talk as I try to think of a topic to bring
up.
Uhhh...
I glance around casually and spot a few guys playing baseball with
a broomstick.
You guys are highschoolers and you're still goofing about like
idiots?
I think to myself, but can't stop watching them.
The pitcher takes  a big wind up and then...

　オレは慌てて、足もとのボール──軟式のテニスボールだった──を拾って、その声に向かって投げた。
*fwomp*

"Thanks!"

I gaze at the scene absentmindedly, while the incomprehensible

...The ball was definitely on course to hit us.
But it didn't.
　とつぜん空中で静止したからだ。

Could it be that...Kotone used her psychic powers?
Psychic powers that she had no control over...
Maybe she managed to use them without even realizing it?
"...It's a little dangerous out here, don't you think?"

"Hm? See what?"
"The ball was headed right for us, but it didn't hit us. Did you see

Then, it wasn't intentional at all.
"Should we go over there to get to a safer place?"
Kotone says while I am lost in my thoughts.
The guys continue to play broomstick baseball.
"Yeah..."
I nod and we both stand up.
We begin to walk to the other bench she points out.
She can't use her powers when she wants to, but she can

It must burn energy to use her powers.
That means using her powers could be a way to prevent an

Interesting... Very interesting...
As that thought goes through my mind...
*bomphf* *bomphf, bomphf*
