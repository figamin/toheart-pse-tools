Classes were done for the day.
I finally have free time.
In this instant, I feel completely alive. I'm definitely going to
be a night owl in the future.
I walk down the hallway and reach the stairs.
I decide to head home early.
I walk down the hallway and reach the shoe racks.
I walk past a teacher and get called out for slacking off.
I leave school and walk down the hill.
I wonder if anyone I know is walking home.
It didn't look like it.
I head home by myself.
It's the night of March 6th.
I get in bed early.
Nothing's more calming than a warm blanket. I can't be the only one
who feels that way, right?
It isn't long before I fall into a peaceful sleep.
