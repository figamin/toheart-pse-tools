４／１９（土）
School was over for that Saturday.
It feels like everyone is in an extra good mood.
I understand why.
It feels great knowing there's no school tomorrow.
　オレは鞄を取って、席から立ち上がった。
I walk down the hallway.
I pass by a few students, but no one that I know.
I walk out into the first floor hallway.
There are a few students standing here and there.
The student council suggestion box is at the bottom of the stairs.
　ひとつ、一般生徒の貴重な意見を入れとくことにする。
"Add more types of sandwiches to the student store." There we go.
I walk out of school and take the usual path down the hill.
　ぺちゃくちゃおしゃべりしながら帰る生徒、自転車で駆け下りていく生徒、様々だ。
What should I do when I get home?
It's the night of April 19th.
I get in bed early.
Nothing's more soothing than warm blankets. I can't be the only one
who feels that way, right?
It's not long before I fall into a pleasant sleep.
