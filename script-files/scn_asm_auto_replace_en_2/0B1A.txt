（綾香　綾香に挑戦　回数イベント１）
（選択肢Ｃ　フェイントを　の続き）
She is clearly much more skilled than me.
Catching her off guard with a surprise attack should be the right
way to handle this.
But there is a high chance Ayaka is waiting for that and will use
it to counter.
In that case, I should go for a feint and follow up quickly with
another attack!
　オレは行く気満々の視線で綾香をにらみ、前に出つつジャブを──。
However, I stop short because it is a feint.
At the same time, Ayaka's gloves twitch a little.
Seeing her reaction, I feel vindicated.
She was preparing to counter my first move!
Immediately, I step forward to throw out my actual attack!
A two-hit combo starting from that fake jab!
The slight delay in time makes Ayaka dish out her counter a split
second too late.
I'm clear!
*swiff* *swiff*
But Ayaka sways away at the last minute.
She dodges it!
My right arm is stretched out too far.
The moment it occurs to me I am in danger...
*swoosh*
　青い閃光が走った。
*SLAM*
A cracking sound.
And then my vision fades out.
　あ～あ…。
She got me good.
I read her moves but didn't have the skills to follow through.
Dammit...
「結構良かったわよ、いまの攻め。
てっきり先手必勝の奇襲攻撃で来ると思って、反撃を狙ってたの。
そこでフェイントとは」
"I thought you'd be easy to deal with as an amateur, but you almost
tricked me there."
"Almost isn't good enough."
"True, but I am a little shocked that I misread you. You made good
use of your amateur status against me."
"Beginner's luck."
"Now you just need to brush up on your basics. Your punch was
woefully amateur."
"Urgh..."
I can't refute that.
I hate to admit it, but she is right.
