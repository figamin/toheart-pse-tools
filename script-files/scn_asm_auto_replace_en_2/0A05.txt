（駅）
I see Masashi in front of the station on my way back.
He looks puzzled, and he is standing in front of an unfamiliar
child.
"Yo, what're you doing?"
「あ、<name>。
じつは…」
"Hey! Calling for backup isn't fair!"
"Wha? Who's this squirt?"
"I'm not a squirt!"
"You're totally a squirt."
"You're too big, is all!"
"And that's why you look like a squirt to me."
「まあまあ」
Masashi cuts into our conversation.
「こいつ、お前の知り合いか？」
"No, first time meeting him."
"Then what's going on?"
I ask.
"Well, I seem to have done something to anger him, not that I have
any recollection of that."
Masashi says, speaking with a genuinely clueless look on his face.
