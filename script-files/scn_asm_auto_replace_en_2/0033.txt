３／３（月）
Phew, a long day of classes is finally over.
Students start to head home, and the classroom is emptying out.
The second floor hallway is crowded with students leaving to go home.
I think I might run into someone I know, but unfortunately I don't.
I descend the stairs to the first floor hallway.
I don't run into anyone in particular.
I leave school.
I guess I'll head home...
March 3rd is over.
I need to get up early again tomorrow.
I'd better go to sleep soon.
