There are so many different types of kids around.
"I guess I love and hate them?"
I can't pick one because not all kids fall under the same category.
"You can't pick both."
"You're putting me in a tough position..."
"Do you love or hate them?"
Lemmy pushes me.
...Okay, I'll give her a straight answer.
"I like the honest kids and hate the naughty ones."
I say frankly.
Lemmy nods, seeming to accept my answer until...
"So which is it?"
She asks.
"Hmmm..."
Looks like this conversation won't end unless I pick one.
　オレの答えは──。
A. I like them.
B. I hate them.
