I see someone unexpected in the hall.
It's Serika Kurusugawa of the famous Kurusugawa family.
What's she up to?
"Hey there, Kurusugawa."
I stand in front of her and smile.
"What brings you here? ...What?
You're here for me?"
Kurusugawa nods.
There's always something odd about her.
It makes her hard to talk to.
"Then what do you need?"
She shows me what she's holding.
"Because?"
It's a deck of cards with eerie illustrations.
They aren't playing cards.
They seem like cards for fortune-telling. Tarot cards, I think.
They seem legit.
"What?!
　これでオレの運勢を占ってくれるって？」
She nods.
I guess that's a nod.
Why does she want to tell my fortune all of a sudden, though?
Hmmm, what should I do?
A. Then tell my fortune.
B. Not now.
