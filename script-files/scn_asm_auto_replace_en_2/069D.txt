（マルチ　回数イベント　１階１）
（４月１４日）
（ＣＧマルチ　廊下掃除）
I go down to the first floor and see a girl with a mop in her hands
cleaning the hallway.
It's Multi the robot. She has sweat on her brow as she cleans with
all her heart.
「…ふぅふぅ」
Multi huffs and puffs, scrubbing the floors.
Robot or not, the sight of her focused and cleaning diligently is
satisfying to watch from afar.
She sighs and appears to take a break, so I approach her.
"Hey, Multi!"
「あ、<name>さん、こんにちは」
She grins.
"Hard at work again, eh. I'm really impressed."
"I love cleaning!"
"You love it? You're such an oddball."
...Or maybe not.
She's a helper robot, so of course she was made for that purpose.
"Anyway, cleaning's a good task to be devoting yourself to. Keep up
the good work."
"I will and thank you. I'll scrub the floors so hard that they shine
and make everyone happy!"
"Now that's what I call motivated!"
I smile.
