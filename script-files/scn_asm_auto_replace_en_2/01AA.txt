（Ｂ　それでも、あるわけないと思う）
"I still don't think so."
I say, making myself perfectly clear.
「べつに理由もなく、かたくなに否定してるわけじゃねーぜ。
すぐに説明できないからといって、なんでもすぐ超常現象のせいにするのは安易すぎるし、無責任だってことさ」
Akari was right that science still can't explain plenty of things,
but that doesn't mean it's supernatural.
If I thought about it more, I'm sure I would be able to come up
with plenty of explanations.
"I'll believe it when there's more evidence."
"Yeah."
"At least ninety percent of supernatural phenomena are just people
imagining things because they think it'd be interesting."
"...Maybe you're right."
I don't want to upset Akari over this.
I decide to drop the subject.
