（Ｂ　そのまま帰宅する。）
"Whatever. I should just go home."
I decide to go home without bothering the mouse.
As I turn around, I see a balloon floating in front of me.
"Gotcha."
I grab the string and pull it back without thinking.
A boy, who looks like he owns the stray balloon, is staring at me.
"Here you go. And don't let go this time!"
As I hand the balloon over, he smiles and disappears into the crowd.
I go straight home after that.
