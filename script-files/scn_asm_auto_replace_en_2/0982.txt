I enter the hall and spot Masashi ahead of me.
"Hey, Masashi."
「あっ、<name>」
"Practice again today?"
"Nah, I'm about to head over to Ajisai for a goodbye party for the
seniors."
"Oh, yeah?"
"Ajisai is an okonomiyaki restaurant in the shopping arcade in front
of the station.
It is known for being tasty, and students from my school often drop
by on the way home.
"Oh yeah, the graduation ceremony is the day after tomorrow."
"We're having the farewell party before then because some of the
seniors will move away straight after graduation."
"Hmmm..."
The seniors haven't been at school since mid-February, so it
already feels as if they had graduated.
"The old folks leave and then new blood arrives again come spring. A
school's circle of life."
「そうだね」
「つーことは、お前ももうすぐ佐藤先輩か」
"Yeah..."
We both already went through the senior phase in middle school, so
it wasn't new.
The excitement isn't really there anymore.
"Oh, sorry. I'm kinda in a rush. The rest of the team already left..."
"Yeah, no worries. Get going."
"See you later."
Masashi waves and runs down the hall.
As he runs, he smiles and waves at the girls who call out to him
and apologizes to a teacher who scolds him for running...before
starting to run again.
Watching Masashi is like watching a scene from a coming-of-age
movie... His life is what adolescence is all about.
