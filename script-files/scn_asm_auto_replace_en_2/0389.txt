（智子　回数イベント　学校を出る　ＬＶ２）
（３月）
（選択肢Ｂ　用があるからの続き）
"I'm busy."
I told her.
"Really?"
Akari looks disappointed.
I tell people, "I'm busy" both when I'm actually busy and when I
just need an excuse to turn someone down.
Akari knows that, so she doesn't bother to ask what I was busy with.
"See you later, then."
Smiling somewhat sadly, she walks off alone.
