（３月８日　朝）
（玄関）
Saturday, March 8th.
If I can make it through today, I'll finally get a break tomorrow.
Keeping that in mind really gets me motivated.
I'm off!
I see a blonde girl at the school gate.
I thought it was someone with dyed blonde hair, but no…
It's Lemmy.
"Hey, Lemmy."
"Hey there, <kata>! You're early today!"
"You really stand out, huh, Lemmy?"
"Well, you don't see a lot of blondes in Japanese school uniforms."
"I feel like I'm used to seeing blonde hair in movies and stuff, but it's so pretty in person."
"Yeah?"
I see it every day, so it feels surprisingly normal to me.
