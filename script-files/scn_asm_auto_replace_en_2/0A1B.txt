（ＳＥ　犬）
　──犬がほえてる。
Urrrgh, all that noise.
"What? It's still so early?"
I may have woken up a bit too early.
A white morning mist still clung to the world outside.
"Maybe I should go back to sleep."
As I am about to clamber back into bed...
Hm?
Did I hear a faint cry for help?
I pull the curtains apart.
「あれ？」
Gentaro is romping around outside.
　ってことは…。
「…やっぱり」
A newspaper delivery girl in a school uniform is trying desperately
to get away from the dog.
"Oh well..."
I quickly change and go outside.
"Hey! When will you ever learn to stop?!"
I leap out in front of the excited Gentaro, who is scampering about
and shaking his tail happily.
His target is definitely...
「あれ？
　
<family>くん？」
"Hey, morning."
"Morning... What're you doing here?"
"My house is right there. As the saying goes, the early bird catches
the worm, right?"
I turn to face Gentaro.
"You broke out of your house again. Go home before they find out."
I ruffle the thick and shaggy fur on his head.
It works, because Gentaro leaves with a pleased look on his face
after that.
「ありがとう、<family>くん…」
"Haha...
オレが彼氏ってのも、あんまり悪かねーだろ？」
「えっ…？」
　冗談っぽく言ったつもりだったが、理緒ちゃんは、一気に耳まで真っ赤になってしまった。
「うん…」
"Anyway, Gentaro seems to really like you."
"Indeed..."
She laughs shyly.
"I'll be done with my deliveries soon. Shall we go somewhere to rest
after that?"
"Sounds good. I'll go along with you for a bit."
"Haha!
ありがとう」
She smiles happily, but when she gets close to a particular,
familiar-looking house...
"Eeeeek!"
She flies right back out with an anguished cry into my arms.
"Whoa! Wh-What happened?!"
"O-Over there..."
"Hm?"
I take a look.
「あ」
It is a giant lizard lumbering across a fenced lawn.
"That's the iguana from back then."
And just like back then, the escaped pet lizard keeps staring at
the trembling Rio's bangs.
It's still after her?
"Don't worry, it can't climb over the fence."
「うん…」
She nods while still hanging on to me.
"But...
こわい…」
「ああ…」
I gently return her embrace.
I can feel her flustered breathing on my chest.
「ね、<family>くん…」
She holds tighter to me.
「大丈夫だってば」
I tighten my grip on her as well in an effort to reassure her.
「<family>くん…
違うの…」
"Hm?"
I glance down at Rio's feet. Where a creature with shaggy fur is
moving right beneath her skirt.
It is Gentaro.
"Hey! You chased us, you little jerk?!"
Despite my angry shouting...
"Woof!"
He barks back happily.
"Aaaaahh!"
Rio gets spooked and starts running away screaming again.
"Noooo! Why meee?!"
The large dog dashes past me with great determination as he
continues his pursuit of Rio.
He's tenacious to a fault, for a dog.
But this is no time for admiration.
"What a pain."
I mumble again and start running as well.
"Heeey!"
I run like crazy and finally catch up to Gentaro.
"Woof!
Woof!"
He turns to look at me and barks happily.
"You little... Take this!"
　そこで一気にダッシュをかけて、そのまま全速力でヤツを抜き去って、理緒ちゃんに追いつく。
"Hold my hand!"
I see that she looks miserable and grab her hand a little roughly.
"Ack!"
The sudden action makes the bag full of newspapers slide down from
her shoulder.
Fortunately, it falls right into my arms instead of scattering all
over the ground.
"Hahaha, sometimes I get lucky too."
I catch the bag.
"Let's go."
I tighten my grip on Rio's hand.
"Ah..."
I start running and pull her along.
Gentaro's barks grow softer and softer.
But I keep on running at full speed.
It feels nice being able to protect Rio like this.
　これから、ずっとこんなふうだって──。
Yes. Everything will start from this point.
And we will keep on running with all our might.
That is what I visualize while running breathlessly... as the
morning mist rises and vanishes into... the clear, blue sky.
