５／１（木）
Alright!
The first day of May is over.
Now I can do what I want.
I leave the classroom and walk down the hallway.
I don't see any
familiar faces.
I walk out into the first floor hallway.
There are a few students standing here and there.
The student council suggestion box is at the bottom of the stairs.
I decide to provide them with my valuable input.
"Add more types of sandwiches to the student store."
There we go.
I walk out of school and take the usual path down the hill.
Some students chat as they walk home while others race by on bikes.
What should I do when I get home?
That night...
I find a brand new notebook in a drawer.
I'd gotten it for free at the grand opening of some store.
Oh, right.
Today's as good a day as any. I might as well start a diary.
"May 1st, Thursday.
I got up in the morning and went to sleep at night."
Now that's a terrible first entry.
Forget it.
I'm not doing this.
Diaries aren't really my thing.
I need to go to bed early to prepare for tomorrow.
Sleep is critical to the development of any child.
Not that I'm a child anymore.
