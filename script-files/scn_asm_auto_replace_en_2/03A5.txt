（智子　回数イベント　帰り道　ＬＶ１）
（４月）
（本屋内）
I stop by the bookstore on the way home and stand around reading a
magazine.
When I look up from the magazine and glance outside...
"y...
　あれは…」
Class Rep happens to be passing by on the street in front of the
store.
When I'm about to put the magazine back on the shelf and go talk to
her, I notice that she's with someone.
　委員長の横には他校の制服を着た男がいて、ふたりいっしょに並んで歩いていた。
Who's that?
Her boyfriend or something?
The guy is talking about something while Class Rep is listening
with her typical poker face.
It doesn't seem like she really wants to be there, more like the
guy is hitting on her and she can't get away.
I want to get a closer look at the guy's face, but they walk past
the store and out of sight.
Mmmm...
I'm surprised to see her in this situation.
And now I'm curious...
A. Follow them.
B. Respect their privacy.
