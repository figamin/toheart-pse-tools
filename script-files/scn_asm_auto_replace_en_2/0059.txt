４／１０（木）
After school...
I have the misfortune of being on cleaning duty.
I'm supposed to clean up the bridge corridor that connects two of
the school buildings.
I take out a broom...
...and sweep here and there before deciding I'm done.
Then I go back to the classroom.
Ugh, that sucked.
The second floor hallway is crowded with students leaving to go
home.
I think I might run into someone I know, but unfortunately I don't.
I walk out into the first floor hallway.
I don't see any familiar faces.
I leave school.
I guess I'll head home...
It's the night of April 10th.
I'm hungry before bed, so I make some instant ramen.
Instant ramen's so good late at night.
*slurp*
