（あかり　好感度イベント９．１）
（Ｂ　買い物はお前にまかせる）
「買い物はお前にまかせる」
I asked.
"What?"
Akari looks a bit surprised.
「でも、さっきはいっしょに行くって…」
"Sure, but think about it. It would be just the two of us shopping
at the supermarket in school uniforms on the way home. If anyone
saw that, Shiho or not, there'd be rumors about us."
"Well...
そうかもね」
Akari hangs her head.
「というわけで、お前ひとりで行ってくれ」
"Got it."
Akari looks disappointed, but she nods.
She's always agreeable.
"By the way, is there anything in particular you want for dinner?"
"Well...
たまにはなんか家庭的なやつが食いたいな。
ファミレスじゃ食えないような」
"What specifically?"
「具体的にとなると、浮かばねーな。
お前の得意料理でいいわ」
"What I'm good at cooking? Hmm, I'm not sure. I don't think I'm
especially good or bad at anything."
「まあ、お前にまかせるよ。
好きなの作ってくれ。
オレ的には、うまくて、安くて、栄養があれば文句ねーから」
"That's a pretty tough ask in and of itself..."
「お前ならできる。
米はある、みそもある、調味料も主要なものはひと通りそろってる。
後はお前を信じる！」
"Okay, got it.
やってみるよ！」
"Alright, that's the spirit!"
It's easier than you might expect to get Akari hyped up.
"So, when should I head over?"
"Some time before six should be fine."
"Okay, got it. See you then."
Akari leaves without me.
