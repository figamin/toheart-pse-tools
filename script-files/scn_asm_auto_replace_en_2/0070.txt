Not that I was going empty-handed.

I leave earlier than usual, but I'm hardly tired at all today.

let me carry it."

Akari?"

"C'mon,
Akari!"

Kids are chatting at the school gate as they wait.
I put Akari's heavy luggage down and decide to sit on the ground rather than join them.
"Ugh, I'm tired...
This is all Akari's fault."

Unnngh...

again.

"Hm?"

Maybe we could get some sunset photos of us."

Now that I think about it, he and I had been through a lot together.
Happy times...
Sad times...
Fun times...
He, Akari, Shiho, and I shared in all those experiences.
I know the four of us won't be together forever,
but I want these relationships to last for at least a while longer.
I want to keep hanging out with them—arguing with Shiho, comforting Akari, and laughing with Masashi.
