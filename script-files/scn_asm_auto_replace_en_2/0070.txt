（雅史エンディング）
（５月６日）
（自室　朝）
The long Golden Week holiday was over, and it was finally the
morning of our school trip.
I was only bringing a single large gym bag.
The less I carried with me, the better.
Not that I was going empty-handed.
The trick was to pack only what was absolutely necessary.
"Let's go!"
I leave earlier than usual, but I'm hardly tired at all today.
「ふぅ…ふぅ…。
待って、<name>ちゃん」
Akari sets her bag on the ground and pauses to catch her breath.
"Man, your bag is huge. You're gonna tire yourself out before the
trip even starts."
It looks like her bag is packed to the gills. It's like she's going
camping.
"But I need this stuff."
"I just gotta suck it up.
お前の荷物よこせ」
"Oh, but..."
「いいから。
お前はかわりにオレの荷物持て」
"It's fine.
ありがとう、<name>ちゃん」
I grab her bag.
"Hngh!"
　一気に持ち上げた。
"Oh, I thought I saw a bag walking with Akari. I guess it was you."
Only one girl would come disparage me first thing in the morning.
It was Shiho, of course.
"Shut up, I'm no bag. And this is Akari's bag, anyway."
"Oh, is it really, Akari?"
"Yeah."
"Ahaha! You try to be ready for even the weirdest things. That's so
'Akari.'"
"Do you think I over-packed?"
"It's probably fine. No shot you'll run short on anything on the
road."
"Right?"
「そう言うお前の荷物はどこなんだ？」
"Mine? I'm not bringing anything."
"Are you nuts? This isn't some kiddy trip. Don't tell me you're
gonna try and get your stuff delivered."
"Well, maybe I am."
"R-Really?"
"Just kidding, that'd be silly. I got to school before you. I just
came out to say hi to you."
"Oh! You had me going for a second there."
「…お前、ついに自分の私生活までデマ化が進んでんのか？
　末期だね」
"What?
　
そっちこそ、ついにユーモアも理解できない石頭になったんじゃない！？」
「お前のユーモアは笑えねーな」
"What?
　
あかりは和んでるでしょ！」
"She was just shocked!"
"Hehe, you must both be excited for the school trip. You have so
much energy."
"Hey!
　あかりっ！」
"Ah!"
Man, if this is what it's like before we even leave, I don't even
want to think about what the trip will be like.
Four tour buses are parked in front of the school.
Kids are chatting at the school gate as they wait.
I put Akari's heavy luggage down and decide to sit on the ground
rather than join them.
"Ugh, I'm tired... This is all Akari's fault."
I sigh and look at the sky. The dazzling May sunlight shines into
my eyes.
Unnngh...
The morning sun is really bright.
I hold up my hand to block it when I happen to see Masashi standing
there.
「──<name>」
"Yeah, later."
"You already look worn out."
"Well, I went through a lot this morning."
I sigh again.
"But, man, spring's almost over."
I mutter.
"We had an eventful spring this year."
"Kind of."
「ねえ──」
Masashi wants to say something.
"Hm?"
「…<name>」
"What?
　あらたまった顔で」
"Get on with it."
Masashi stares at me for a bit.
「──僕たち、ずっと、友だちだよね？」
He asks.
"*sigh...*
　なに言ってんだ、いまさら。
オレたち１０年来の親友じゃねーか」
"That's true."
Masashi grins.
"Hey! Time to assemble!"
Mr. Kibayashi shouts from a ways away.
「行こうか、<name>」
"Sure."
I stand up and walk with him.
「ねえ、<name>は北海道、楽しみ？」
Masashi asks me.
"Just a hunch.
なんせ初めて行くんだし、期待もふくらむぜ。
とくに函館の夜景なんか楽しみだな」
"I want to see the view of the horizon from Mt. Hakodate."
"Yeah, you don't get views like that around here."
"Okay."
"Watching the sunset could be cool. Maybe we could get some sunset
photos of us."
"Sounds good."
Now that I think about it, he and I had been through a lot together.
Happy times...
Sad times...
Fun times...
He, Akari, Shiho, and I shared in all those experiences.
I know the four of us won't be together forever, but I want these
relationships to last for at least a while longer.
I want to keep hanging out with them—arguing with Shiho, comforting
Akari, and laughing with Masashi.
Those are the greatest times of my life.
"What are you thinking about?"
「…いや、べつに」
I smile and shake my head.
"Anyway, let's enjoy the next five days!"
"Yep."
