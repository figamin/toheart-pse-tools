（芹香　日付イベント１）
（３月４日）
（１時間目の休み時間）
（廊下）
During first period break…
I head to the junior's classrooms with the Black Magic Compendium in hand.
I ask a junior in the hall which class Kurusugawa is in and learn she is in 2-A.
I go straight to Class 2-A.
Unfortunately, it's completely deserted.
They probably went to another classroom for their next class.
Oh well. I'll have to come back another time.
When I'm about to leave...
*clatter*
I hear something move.
"Huh?"
I turn and see a lone girl standing in the classroom.
"What?"
It was Kurusugawa.
What, she's here?
And it looks like she's been here the whole time.
She's crouching on the floor where I can't see her.
What is she doing here on her own?
Well, whatever, I'll just give her book back.
"Kurusugawa."
I call her name as I enter.
"Hm?"
She hears and turns toward me.
"…"
She stares at me.
"Hi, I'm the freshman who bumped into you this morning. Do you remember?
Sorry about that."
I smile.
"…"
"So what are you doing here?
Everyone else left already."
She paused to think for three seconds,
before replying,
"I'm looking for something."
"For what?
A book?"
I ask.
"…"
She nods.
"Would it happen to be the Black Magic Compendium?"
I grin.
"…"
"What?
You want to know how I knew?
Heheh, well, behold!"
I show her the book I'm hiding behind my back.
"I have it!"
"…"
"You probably dropped it when I bumped into you,
and it ended up with me.
Here, you can have it back."
I hand the book to her.
"With how hard you're looking for it, I'm guessing it's important to you."
She nods.
"Then try not to lose it again.
Well, you can blame me for that, really. Hahaha."
"…"
She stares blankly at me.
Hmmm...
She's as weird as ever.
She's got this unique vibe about her.
And I can't deny...
She's pretty when I see her up close.
I guess that's to be expected of a rich girl.
It's like every part of her body was made to order.
She's too beautiful, almost like a doll.
The way she spaces out is a bit odd though.
"So you're into...
the occult?"
She nods.
"That's weird.
Are a lot of...
rich girls...
into weird stuff?"
"…"
"What?
You don't understand?
Huh, I see.
Aren't you in the Occult Research Club?"
She moves her head up and down.
"I don't know much about it, but I hear you do black magic and spirit channeling and stuff.
Do you actually?
Well?
Do you?!"
"Yes."
Kurusugawa answers sincerely.
"Your club's goal is to 'revive esoteric disciplines that have been lost because of science,' right?"
She moves her head up and down.
"That's what it said on the club list we got when we started as freshman."
I thought it was sketchy at the time, to be honest, but I'm also a little curious.
"Did you come up with that description for the club?"
She shakes her head.
Evidently, the answer was "no."
"No?"
She nods.
"Oh, the club president thought of it?
I see, you're not the president.
…Oh?
The president is a distinguished professor of the occult, but hardly ever shows up to the club?
Hahaha, like a ghost in the occult club? That's funny!"
"…"
"Sorry."
Just then, I realize we only have another minute until the next class starts.
Uh-oh, all my questions are keeping her here.
We'll be late for class.
I don't care what that does to me, but Kurusugawa's teacher might get mad at her.
"Well, it's almost time for class.
You ought to get moving.
Your teacher's gonna be mad if you're late."
*nod*
She nods.
"See you tomorrow."
I wave and turn to leave when…
*tug*
"Hm?"
She grabs my jacket to stop me.
"Huh?
What?"
"…"
Kurusugawa murmurs something.
"What?
You want to know if I'm into the occult?"
She nods.
"Well…"
Honestly, I have no belief in the supernatural whatsoever.
I feel it would be nice if such things were real, but I fundamentally believe otherwise.
But that isn't to say I have no interest.
If it's real, I want to see it myself. I wanted to experience it.
I don't believe in Santa Claus, but I think the world would be better if he was real.
"Yeah, I'm interested in it."
So that's my answer.
Kurusugawa whispers something...
It's barely audible again.
"What?
Will I come check out the club? 
Yeah, sure,
if I have time."
I smile.
"…"
Kurusugawa blushes and hangs her head for some reason.
The bell chimes.
"Oh crap! I'm gonna be late!
See you later, Kurusugawa."
I wave and left Class 2-A.
