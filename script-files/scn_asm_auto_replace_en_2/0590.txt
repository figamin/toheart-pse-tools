（葵　回数イベント５　選択後　Ａ）
"You sure are impressive, Aoi."
"Oh, d-do you really think so?"
"You work hard every day from morning to night. I really respect
that."
Aoi smiles bashfully in response.
「わ、私って、基本的に才能ないですから、なにをするにも人一倍努力しないと駄目なんです。
だから──」
"No talent? That's definitely not true."
I cut her off.
「え？」
"You do have talents. Not finding working hard a chore is a great
talent in itself."
"The ability to work hard is a talent?"
"Yeah...
ほら、中国の孔子も言ってんだろ？
これを知る者、これを好む者にしかず。
これを好む者、これを楽しむ者にしかずってさ」
"It means that you can't beat someone who loves what they do. So you
must have a talent for martial arts if you truly enjoy practicing
it."
"I-Is it...?"
"You enjoy martial arts, right?"
"Oh, yes."
Aoi gives a big nod.
"Then that's a bona fide talent of yours."
"Oh..."
"So you should have more confidence. You're my teacher and all."
I give Aoi a pat on the shoulder and she drops her head bashfully
before looking up at me.
"Yes, I think I am starting to feel more confident! Thank you very
much!"
She grins.
Practice continues until the evening that day.
As we're leaving, Aoi gives me a book about martial arts to borrow.
「格闘技における基本的なことがひととおり書かれているんです。
これを読めば、きっとなにかの参考になると思いますよ」
"Ooh, thanks. I'll give it a read."
I take the book, and Aoi beams with joy.
