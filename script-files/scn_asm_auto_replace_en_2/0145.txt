It's the second period break.
I'm thirsty, so I buy a cafe au lait from the vending machine. I'm drinking it when…
"<name>-chan."
Akari says something to me.
"Did you get a drink?"
"Yeah, I think you can see that."
"Oh, cafe au lait again?"
"Why not? I like it."
"…"
"What? Do you want me to buy you one too?"
"N-No, but..."
Just then…
"Oh!"
"It's Kurusugawa."
Kurusugawa passes by us looking as spacey as ever.
Her wavy black hair sways in the wind.
I wave to her and grin.
"…"
I don't know if she notices. Her face remains blank and she continues on without responding.
It looks like she's heading to the courtyard.
Maybe she's going to sunbathe.
"She sure is pretty."
Akari watches her go and whispers.
"Huh, even girls think she's pretty?"
"Yeah, of course.
I'd love to have such shiny hair."
"I see.
Sure, she's pretty."
"So you think so too?"
"Obviously.
Guys are more into long hair than girls are."
"Huh, really."
"I mean, some guys like short hair, but I'm sure even those guys can appreciate some long, pretty hair."
"Hmm..."
Akari plays with her pigtails and awkwardly nods.
After a moment's silence, Akari asks me something.
"Hey, <name>-chan,
do you like straight hair?"
"Sure."
"What about curly hair?"
"I like it."
"You like long hair?"
"Yeah."
"And short hair?"
"Love it."
"Geez,
is there any hairstyle you don't like?"
"If it's cute and looks good on the girl,
I guess I don't really care."
"That doesn't tell me anything!"
