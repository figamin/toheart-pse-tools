３／１８（火）
期末テスト４日目
I walk down the hallway.
I pass by a few students, but no one that I know.
I descend the stairs to the first floor hallway.
I don't run into anyone in particular.
I walk out of school and take the usual path down the hill.
　ぺちゃくちゃおしゃべりしながら帰る生徒、自転車で駆け下りていく生徒、様々だ。
I guess I'll go home and study today.
Tomorrow is finally the last day of exams.
All that remains is the Japanese exam.
I decide to memorize some kanji.
