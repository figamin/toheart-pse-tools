After school...
Some students hurry home while others stick around to chat and gossip.
I pick up my bag and rise from my chair.
I walk down the hall as I gaze at the view outside.
The scenery in the distance seems white and hazy.
The view would be better from the roof, but this is still pretty nice.
I descend the stairs to the first floor hallway.
I don't run into anyone in particular.
I head home by myself.
Thinking about how alone I am makes me feel a bit sad.
Aren't any of my friends around?
I get used to my new class in no time.
I'm a pretty adaptable guy.
But getting too comfortable will make me so relaxed that I'll fall asleep in class.
I decide to go to sleep early to avoid that.
April 9th comes to an end.
