３／１０（月）
After school...
The school comes alive like it had woken up from a slumber.
I pick up my bag as my classmates laugh and joke around me.
I trudge down the hallway.
I pass by students on their way home.
I would've liked to see someone I knew, but I don't. I reach the stairs.
"Later, <family>"
"See you later."
I wave to a boy in my class and leave school.
I head home by myself.
Thinking about how alone I am makes me feel a bit sad.
Aren't any of my friends around?
My next door neighbors, Mr. and Mrs. Kawakami, are fighting again tonight.
Somehow, just like clockwork, they always wait until late at night to start fighting.
They're the kind of couple who fights a lot because of how close they are, so I'm not worried about them, but I wish they would try not to bother the neighbors with it.
