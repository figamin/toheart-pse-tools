（４月１６日　朝）
（自分の部屋）
Wednesday, April 16th.
"Whoa."
As soon as I open the curtains, bright morning light floods the room.
It's brutal on my freshly opened eyes.
But it does wake me right up.
"I was worried you might oversleep and we'd be late."
"Even if I did,
I'd be able to make it to school on time if I was alone."
"That's mean..."
It had been chilly for a while, but now it's getting pretty warm,
if not hot.
I guess that makes this the height of spring.
