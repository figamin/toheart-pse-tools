（好感度イベント１１を見ているとき）
（４月２７日　ＰＭ２時）
I end up wandering around town.
After having lunch at a random restaurant, I head back out onto the street, and...
"Oh?"
Someone in the plaza draws my attention.
She has long, straight hair.
She has a pretty young-looking face, so I assume she's either my age or a little younger.
I can't remember who she is, but I know she's familiar.
She's pretty cute.
And her figure is especially nice.
She looks like a model you might see in a magazine.
Especially with the way her sweater highlights her chest.
Any guy would want to look at her.
The girl seems to be waiting for something. She has a kind of pouty look on her face and is gazing at the sky with disinterest.
She looks at the clock on the street.
She sighs.
Apparently, she's been waiting for a while.
And then…
"…"
"…"
Our eyes happen to meet.
She looks surprised for a moment.
"…!"
Then, she frantically looks away.
What?
Seems like she doesn't want me seeing her.
Weird.
So, I have met her somewhere, right?
I squint and watch her.
She looks at me, then away again, then returns to gazing up at the sky, acting like I'm not there.
However, her luck had run out.
When I see her face from the side, I immediately realize who she is.
"Ohhh, Class Rep!"
"…!"
The Class Rep...
shudders.
I always see her from the side in class.
I'd know that profile anywhere.
It's the Class Rep.
"What, so it's you?"
I laugh as I walk over to her.
"Hey, are you wearing contacts today?
Between that and the straight hair, I couldn't tell it was you till I got closer."
"…"
She doesn't seem to know how to feel about all this.
"Hm?
Something wrong?"
"N-Not really."
She says,
sighing.
"You live around here, right?
Guess it makes sense that we'd run into each other on a weekend."
She mutters to herself.
"So, I see you're all dressed up. Who are you waiting for?"
"What?"
"You're waiting for someone, right?"
"N-Not really, no."
"Oh really?
Sounds fishy.
Is it someone you don't want me to know about?"
"Err..."
It seems like I was right. She looks conflicted and hesitates to speak.
"So who is it?"
I ask teasingly.
"Wh-Why does it matter?"
"The more you hide it, the more I wanna know.
Is it your boyfriend?"
"I-I told you before... I don't have a boyfriend."
"Then who?
Hmm..."
"Doesn't matter.
If that's all you have to ask, then go away."
She puts a hand on my chest and pushes me away.
"Aww, you're trying to ditch me when we were lucky enough to run into each other? You're no fun.
Oh yeah, by the way..."
"What?"
The Class Rep glares at me. She seems annoyed.
"You're pretty cute without glasses too."
"…"
"And those clothes and that hairstyle look good on you."
"Wh-Why are you telling me this?"
"Just wanted to tell you what I think."
"I-I can't believe you just said that."
"Anyway, now I'm really curious about who you're meeting up with."
"Geez, <family>-kun, enough already!"
She blushes.
Haha...
I like her usual indifferent demeanor, but this vibe is nice in a different way.
She was less "Class Rep" and more "Tomo".
Typically, she was plain and didn't stand out much. Looking at her now, she's actually real pretty.
While we're talking...
"Sorry, sorry... I'm awfully late."
I hear someone else who sounds like they're from Kansai. I turn and see a refined businessman standing there.
"Just had a lot of work to deal with."
"..."
When the Class Rep sees him, she looks down and presses a hand against her face.
"Oh, what's wrong, Tomoko?
　
With a friend today?"
T-Tomoko?
"You have a boyfriend you want to introduce to your dad?"
D-Dad?
So this handsome old guy is her dad?
"Nope,
we just happened to run into each other here.
He's a classmate, not my boyfriend."
She rushes to tell her dad that we're only classmates.
Does she have to deny it so hard, though?
I make eye contact with her dad.
"Oh, nice to meet you. I'm <family>."
I bow and introduce myself.
First impressions are important.
"I'm Hoshina's father. Nice to meet you too."
He responds in kind.
Oh?
Didn't she mention something about her parents being divorced before?
And she's living with her mom?
She said her dad's still back in Kobe.
"Dad happened to be here on a business trip, so we planned to meet up."
The Class Rep guesses what was on my mind and explains.
"Huh, really?"
What?
Why couldn't she just say she was waiting for her dad?
"Want to get going?
Do you want to come with, <family>-kun?
　
We can grab something to eat."
"What?!"
"D-Dad…;"
"No, I wouldn't want to interrupt your family time."
"No?
　
Maybe another time then."
"Uh, sure."
"B-Bye, <family>-kun."
"Yeah, see you at school."
They walked to the taxi stand by the station.
Hmmm...
I see a side of the Class Rep I never knew about.
This was an experience to remember.
