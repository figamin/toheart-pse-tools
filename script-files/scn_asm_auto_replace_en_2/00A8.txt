（３月１４日　朝）
（玄関）
Friday, March 14th.
Final exams start today.
Half of me is upset about it, but the other half is just glad school ends early.
I stayed up studying until last night, so I'm a little tired.
"I hope you do well on the test."
"Well, I studied as much as I could.
Now I just have to pray that the questions I went over last night are actually on the exam."
"Good luck."
"You too."
