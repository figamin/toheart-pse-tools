（イベントなしの場合）
（自分の部屋）
Sunday, March 23rd.
I wake up at noon.
I just know I need to eat.
I don't know what to do with the rest of the day.
There's no point in staying home, so I decide to wander around town.
Maybe I can find something to do.
I come to the station and think about where to go from there.
A. Go to the arcade.
B. Go to the shopping arcade.
C. Go to the barber.
