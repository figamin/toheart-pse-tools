４／１６（水）
After school...
I have the misfortune of being on cleaning duty.
I'm supposed to clean up the classroom.
"Hey, outta the way!"
You don't need to be here. Get out already!
"I said move it!"
I shoo the chatting students away as I sweep.
Alright, all done.
Ugh, that sucked.
Maybe I'll go straight home.
I walk down the hallway and reach the stairs.
I decide to head home early.
I walk out into the first floor hallway.
I don't see any
familiar faces.
The student council suggestion box is at the bottom of the stairs.
I decide to provide them with my valuable input.
"Add more options to the school lunch menu."
There we go.
I walk out of school and take the usual path down the hill.
Some students chat as they walk home while others race by on bikes.
What should I do when I get home?
As I'm about to get in bed that night...
"Oh!"
Everything rumbles and shakes.
Oooh...
That was a pretty big earthquake.
Maybe I'll check the news.
I turn the TV on.
Not even a minute later...
*beep, beep*
A news ticker scrolls across the screen.
"The magnitude in each region is as follows…"
Everything shook
pretty badly.
Gotta be careful since there have been a lot of earthquakes lately.
But I probably wouldn't make it out of that in one piece either.
I look at the clock.
Ack, it's already two.
Even the bad kids are asleep by now.
