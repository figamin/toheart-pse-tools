４／１３（日）
That night...
I'm watching TV in the living room when I see the most incredible
scene.
It's a colorless business district.
I can feel how hollow and artificial it all is.
Then, countless women elegantly descend from the sky.
They land as softly as feathers and each enters one of the cold
buildings.
The instant they pass through the automatic doors, their outfits
magically transform into business attire, and they begin to perform
office duties as if they've been working there for a while.
Color floods the offices.
People smile.
At the end, the words "Kurusugawa HM Series" appear on screen.
It's a commercial for Robomaids.
It's a highly competitive industry, and manufacturers are
constantly fighting for a bigger market share, so they have to
spend big money on their image and branding.
How much did it cost to produce that commercial alone?
I can't even imagine.
I wonder if Multi will be in a commercial like this one day.
Hahaha, if she came down from the sky, I'd be worried about her
landing safely.
