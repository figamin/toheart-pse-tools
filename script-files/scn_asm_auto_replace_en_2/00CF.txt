（４月１３日　昼）
（自分の部屋）
I wake up around noon.
There isn't a cloud in the sky.
　下に降りて、冷蔵庫の中のもので、適当な朝飯
（昼だけど）を作って食べた。
But now, I have a problem.
I don't have anything to do.
That being the case, I feel like doing something I wouldn't do
normally.
"Alright!"
I yank the futon blanket off my bed and throw it out the window to
air it out.
I go downstairs to grab a futon beater, then climb up to the roof
myself.
I spread out the blanket on the roof and beat it.
I can't remember how many years it's been since I did this myself.
*bam, bam, bam*
After some thorough beating, sweat drips from my brow.
Now if I air the futon blanket out for the rest of the day, it'll
smell nice and fresh by night.
In the meantime, I watch some videos I'd been meaning to check out.
I end up watching TV all afternoon.
