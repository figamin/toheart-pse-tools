（中庭）
I walk out into the courtyard.
"Hey."
Kurusugawa turns towards me.
"Sunbathing again?"
She nods.
"You sure do like this place. Mind if I sit next to you?"
She nods.
I sit down next to Kurusugawa.
The sky is clear, and the weather is great.
The gentle sunlight and pleasant breeze feel nice.
I can kind of understand why Kurusugawa is always sunbathing here.
"Hmmm..."
I stretch and look to my side.
Kurusugawa is gazing up at the sky.
I can't tell if she's dozing off or simply doing what she always
does.
There isn't much time for me to spend with Kurusugawa.
She'll be picked up and driven home in less than an hour.
This time is precious.
"She really is beautiful.
I can't help but admire her when I look at her like this.
Her hair blowing in the wind, her dainty lips, her clear skin...
it's all perfect.
　生まれの違いってやつかね。
I normally find this level of beauty kind of off-putting, but
Kurusugawa is so quirky and awkward that I don't feel that way.
　つまり、見た目はきれいな美人系だけど、中身はかわいい系というか…。
Kurusugawa is spacing out, as usual.
