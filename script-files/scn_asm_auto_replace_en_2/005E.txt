４／１５（火）
School's over and students are clearing out of the classroom.
I decide to get going too.
I walk down the hall as I gaze at the view outside.
The scenery in the distance seems white and hazy.
The view would be better from the roof, but this is still pretty
nice.
I reach the shoe racks.
I look for love letters but don't find any.
Oh well.
I climb up and down this hill every day.
I wonder if it's making my legs stronger.
I bet it would feel good to speed down the hill on a skateboard.
It's the night of April 15th.
*rattle, rattle*
I open a window to get some fresh air.
*whoooooooosh*
Then, I hear a loud sound.
I look outside and see a red light in the night sky.
It's moving slowly.
　あの高さからして、戦闘機だな。
Maybe it's out for some late night flight practice.
It would've been neat if it was actually a UFO though.
No, that's dumb. Who am I? Shiho?
I watch until the light was out of sight.
I can make out faint gray lines in the moonlight.
Vapor trails look pretty cool at night.
