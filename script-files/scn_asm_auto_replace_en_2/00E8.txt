（駅前）
That afternoon, I wander around the station area.
After walking for a while...
"Oh."
I try to walk past the woman in front of me and accidentally bump her shoulder.
"Sorry about that."
"Oh, it's fine!"
She didn't seem to mind it. The woman keeps walking.
Hm? Wait a sec…
Her voice sounds familiar.
"Wait…"
Something's sitting on the ground at my feet.
It's a card holder.
I pick it up and look inside to find a train pass.
The name on the card was Mineko Karashima.
Mineko Karashima?
That's the same name as the host of a radio show I listen to all the time.
Could it be her?!
Seriously?
At any rate, I decide to return it to her and ask.
I follow behind her and announce myself.
"Excuse me?"
"Yes?"
"Um, is this yours?"
I show her the card holder.
"Oh! Yes, it is!

What? When did I drop that?!"
"Probably when I bumped into you."
"Oh, maybe.
Thanks."
Her voice is exactly the same as on the radio.
It has to be her.
"Um, are you Mine?"
"What?"
She looks shocked.
Oh crap.
Do I have the wrong person?
But a moment later...
"Oh, yes. That's me..."
She responds like she's wondering who I am.
"Whoa, seriously?"
"Err..."
"Oh, I'm just a fan.
I love your show."
"Really? Thank you."
"Uh, can I have an autograph?"
"Okay, sure."
"Yay!
In that case…"
I take a notebook and pen out of my bag and hand them to Mine.
"Write it on the cover as big as possible."
"But aren't you using this for school?"
"It's fine, go for it."
"Really?
　
Alright then…"
She takes the pen and signs her name.
Oh, she has excellent handwriting.
She must do this a lot.
I guess your signature is something that improves with practice.
"Here you are."
"Awesome! Thank you."
"This is the first time I've met a listener like this.
Did you know who I was right away?"
"No, I just saw your name on the card
and noticed you sound like her."
"I just act like myself on the radio."
"So you're that goofyin real life?"
"What?
I'm not that goofy."
"You so are."
"You think so? Hehe."
With a conflicted smile, Mine put the card carrier in her bag.
"I'm actually recording a show today!"
"Is the studio in this neighborhood?!"
"No, nowhere near it."
Then why are you here?
What a mystery.
"Um, can I ask for one more thing?"
"Yes."
"Can I have a handshake?"
"Oh, yes, that's fine."
I shake Mine's hand.
"Good luck with the show."
"Thank you,
I hope you give it a listen.
See you later."
"Bye."
Mine waves goodbye and leaves.
Wow, I met a celebrity.
And I got her to sign my notebook too.
Alright.
I'm bragging about this to everyone tomorrow.
