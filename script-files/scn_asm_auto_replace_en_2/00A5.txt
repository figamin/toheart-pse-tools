（３月１１日　朝）
（玄関）
Tuesday, March 11th.
"I'm going to school now!"
I inform the empty living room I'm leaving, then head out and lock the door.
"So then Shiho went and…"
We take the usual path up the hill. Akari goes on about something I don't care about until...
"Oh, it's Hoshina."
Akari pauses her story to point someone out.
"Hoshina?"
I look and see a girl with braided hair and glasses walking next to us.
Oh, her?
That's the annoying girl I met at the library.
"Good morning."
Akari greets her.
"…"
Hoshina just glances over without saying anything as she passes by.
"Isn't she in your class?"
"Yeah,
she's the class representative."
"But she didn't even say hi to you."
"Y-Yeah.
We're not that close, to be honest."
Akari says, her lips curling into a wry smile.
