（３月１１日　朝）
（玄関）
Tuesday, March 11th.
"I'm going to school now!"
I inform the empty living room I'm leaving, then head out and lock
the door.
「──でね、そのとき、志保がぁ…」
We take the usual path up the hill. Akari goes on about something I
don't care about until...
"Oh, it's Hoshina."
Akari pauses her story to point someone out.
"Hoshina?"
I look and see a girl with braided hair and glasses walking next to
us.
Oh, her?
That's the annoying girl I met at the library.
"Good morning."
Akari greets her.
"Hoshina just glances over without saying anything as she passes by.
「たしか、お前と同じクラスなんだよな？」
"Thanks!
クラスの委員長なんだ」
「…でも、あいつ、お前になんのあいさつもしなかったぜ？」
"O-Okay.
正直言うと、保科さんとは、そんなに親しくもないんだ」
Akari says, her lips curling into a wry smile.
